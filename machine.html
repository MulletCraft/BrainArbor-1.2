What is Machine Learning?

Machine learning: is behind chatbots and predictive text, language translation apps, the shows Netflix suggests to you, and how your social media feeds are presented. It powers autonomous vehicles and machines that can diagnose medical conditions based on images.

Impacts:

Machine learning: has had a significant impact on the financial services industry, enabling more accurate predictions and better risk management. With the use of algorithms and predictive models, financial institutions are able to make data-driven decisions that lead to increased profitability and improved customer experience.
